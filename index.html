About Me

Hi, I’m Vijay Pujar. Currently, I’m in my final year of the LLB (Hons.) program at the National Law School of India University (NLSIU), Bengaluru. I also hold a degree in Civil Engineering from the National Institute of Engineering (NIE), Mysore.

After graduation, I pursued the Civil Services Examination, where I cleared multiple Mains and appeared for interviews. That journey deepened my understanding of law, governance, and public systems and sparked my eventual transition to legal education.

Outside of law, I’ve studied data science and have been an Educator where I have taught hundreds of aspirants preparing for civil services. I believe in continuous learning, thoughtful work and connecting law with real-world impact.
